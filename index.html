Job Started

You parked correctly at your first house. Time to grab the sprayer.

Controls: Move on foot and steer with WASD or Arrow Keys. Choose truck speed in mph from the phone slider (or [/]) and the truck maintains that speed. Press E to enter/exit truck and use tools. Press P while driving to quick-park in front of the nearest house. Click left/right in the driving view, or use Q / F, to rotate what you're looking at, and press R to reset behind the van. Press M to view the full area map. Press T to toggle Trainer Mode. During service hold Space and paint with the mouse circle (wheel adjusts concentration/coverage size), and press E near kids/dog toys to move them before spraying. Walking up to a house wall switches to first-person view until you back away. Use phone app tabs to switch between Messaging, Email, and Field Management. In Phone Field Management, select assigned customer and Time In, then add a tech comment and Time Out when done. Phone has a Controls button in the top-right panel.
